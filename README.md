## About

I design and ship **production AI systems** — LLM applications, multi-agent workflows, RAG platforms, and FastAPI backends that stay reliable under real load.

My work sits at the intersection of **model capability** and **system engineering**: fine-tuning when needed, retrieval when grounding matters, agents when workflows get complex, and clean APIs when products need to scale.

I care about architecture, evaluation, latency, cost, and outcomes — not demos that break in production.

## Focus Areas

| Area | What I deliver |
|------|----------------|
| **Multi-Agent Systems** | Tool-using agents, orchestration, and reliable task workflows |
| **LLM & RAG Platforms** | Grounded generation with vector search, re-ranking, and eval loops |
| **Model Adaptation** | Fine-tuning with LoRA / QLoRA / PEFT for domain-specific performance |
| **AI Backend Services** | FastAPI services for chat, document intelligence, and AI product APIs |
| **Cloud AI Stack** | OpenAI, Gemini, Vertex AI, and scalable deployment on AWS / GCP |
